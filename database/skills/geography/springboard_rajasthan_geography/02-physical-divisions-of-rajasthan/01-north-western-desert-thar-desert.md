Note:- 25 cm Isohyets :- It divides the desert into two parts which are arid and semi-arid desert.

Arid Desert/Rathi Region :-

’āś Less than 25 cm rainfall region known as Rathi Region. ’āś Arid desert again divided in two parts.

Without Sand Dunes Area With Sand Dunes Area (41.5 %) (58.5%)

(a) Without Sand Dunes Area :-

’āś Sand dunes are not fount in this region, because 'Rocky Desert' is found here. ’āś Rocky Desert is called Hamada. It's extension maximum in Jaisalmer (Pokaran, Lodarava and Ramgarh), Barmer, Jodhpur and Phalodi.

(b) With Sand Dunes Area :-

’āś When the wind deposits soil then formed structure is known as sand dunes. ’āś In Rajasthan State sand dunes are maximum in Jaisalmer. ’āś All types sand dune are found in Jodhpur.

’āś When the winds deposit soil in crescent shape then formed structure is called barkhan. ’āś The direction from which the wind comes in Barkhan has a convex slope, while in the forward direction there is a concave slope. ’āś The width of the barkhan is 100 to 200 meter while the height is 10-20 meter. ’āś These sand dunes are mostly found in Shekhawati (Maximum Bhaleri Village - Churu) ’āś Barkhan Sand dunes maximum contribute in desertification because these sand dunes are maximum dynamic.

2. Transverse Sand Dune :-

’āś When the soil is deposited at the right angle of wind, then formed structure is called transverse sand dune. ’āś These sand dunes are mostly found in Bikaner, Shriganganagar, Hanumangarh and Shekhawati Region.

3. Longitudinal/Linear Sand Dune :-

’āś When the soil is deposited parallel to the wind, then formed structure is called longitudinal/linear sand dune. ’āś These sand dunes are mostly found in Jaisalmer.

4. Star shaped sand dune :-

’āś Star types sand dunes are formed in a sandy desert when the direction of the wind changes a lot. These dunes have three or more "arms". ’āś These sand dunes are mostly found in Bikaner, Jaisalmer, Suratgarh (Sriganganagar). 5. Parabolic Sand dune :-

’āś Sand dunes which found opposite to Barkhan or hair Clip shape sand dunes are called parabolic. ’āś These sand dunes are maximum found in Rajasthan State.

6. Seif Sand Dunes :-

’āś When is change in direction of wind during formation of Barkhan, then barkhan one arm get extend, then formed structure is known as seif.

Note :- 1. Barkhan is like transverse sand dunes. 2. Seif is like Longitudinal sand dunes.

’āś The small sand dunes formed near bushes and small vegetation is called a Shrub Coppies. ’āś Note:- Nebkha - It is a sand dunes which formed around vegetation or back side of bushes.

(2) Semi - Arid Desert / Bangar Region

’āś The physical division which is located between Arid Desert and Arawali region is known as semi Arid Desert or a physical Division which is located between 25cm. Isohyet Line and 50cm Isohyet Line Rainfall line is called Semi Arid Desert. ’āś According to the study Semi Arid Desert again divides into 4 parts. It's extension in Jalore, Sanchor, Pali, Barmer, Balotra, Sirohi and Jodhpur. ’āś In this Basin saline plants (Halophyte) are found in Barmer, Balotra. ’āś "Rann of Nehar" (Jalore) is located in this basin. ’āś "Kala Bhura Dhungar" is located in Pali to the east of this basin. (b) Nagauri Upland :- ’āś The brine water lakes are the highest found in Nagaur. ’āś The most fluoride region where fluorosis disease is more common, therefor this region is called hump belt or banka belt. It's situated between Nagaur & Ajmer.

’āŠ Desert Related Other Important Points (1) Khadeen / Playa Lakes

’āś Temporary lakes are called khadeen/Palaya lakes in northern Jaisalmer. ’āś In These lakes khadeen agriculture was starded by Paliwal Brahmins. ’āś Rabi Crops is mainly sown in these lakes.

’āś In desert Marshy, saline and infertile land is called Rann / Taat. ’āś These are Maximum found in Jaisalmer(1st ) and Barmer(2nd ).

(3) Water Belt (Lathi Series) :-

’āś Remaining underground water of Saraswati river is known as water belt/Lathi series. ’āś It is located between Pokaran and Mohangarh in Jaisalmer. ’āś Chandan tubewells are called "Pot of Thar", which is located on lathi series in chandan village.

NOTE :- Second water belt of Rajasthan = Baytu (Balotra) to Sanchore .

(4) Akal Wood Fossil Park (Jaisalmer):-

’āś The oldest wooden remains in Jaisalmer (National Desert Park) which are currently received as stone form. ’āś The formation period of these fossils is the Jurassic Period. (Before 180 Million years)

(5) Baap Boulder Clay :- ’āś Sediments and large stones / Boulders formed by deposition from glaciers / ice sheets are called Boulder clay. ’āś The formation period of Boulder clay is the Permo - Carboniferous (250-280 million year ago).

’āś These Boulder Clay is mostly found in Phalodi,Jodhpur (Baap).
