General Introduction  Rajasthan is a backward state of the country in terms of industries.  The main reason for this is considered to be underdevelopment of infrastructure and climate.  Kota is called the industrial city of Rajasthan due to rapid industrial development.  Rajasthan ranks 10th in the country in terms of industries.  Presently in Rajasthan :- 1- Jaipur has the highest Industries / Factories in rajasthan. 2- Alwar has the highest number of big industries in rajasthan
