The Information Technology (IT) and electronics sectors are specialized industries focused on the production and management of high-tech goods and digital services.

## Industry Scope

The electronics industry is characterized by the manufacturing of various hardware components and consumer devices. Key products within this sector include:

- Telephones
- Computers
- Other electronic devices

While Information Technology and electronics are closely related, they represent distinct categories of manufacturing and service-oriented activities within the broader industrial landscape.

**Key facts:**
- **Electronics industry products:** Includes telephones and computers.
- **Distinction:** The sector is categorized separately from heavy industries like steel or aluminium smelting.
